## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the library system website
2. Click "Register" in the top navigation
3. Fill out the registration form:
   - **Email**: Your institutional email address
   - **Password**: Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**: Your full name
   - **User Type**: Select your role (Student, Faculty, Staff)
   - **ID Number**: Your student/employee ID
   - **Department**: Your academic department or work unit
   - **Phone** (optional): Contact number
   - **Address** (optional): Mailing address

4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for verification instructions (if required)
6. Your account will be activated and you can log in

### Logging In

1. Go to the library system homepage
2. Click "Sign In" 
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"

You'll be redirected to the main dashboard based on your user type.

## User Types and Permissions

### Students
- Browse and search the book catalog
- View book details and availability
- Check out books (up to 5 simultaneously)
- Renew books (up to 2 times if no reservations)
- Make reservations for unavailable books
- View personal circulation history
- Update profile information

### Faculty/Staff
- All student permissions
- Extended checkout periods (4 weeks vs 2 weeks)
- Higher checkout limits (up to 10 books)
- Priority in reservation queues
- Access to faculty-only collections

### Library Staff
- All faculty permissions
- Manage book transactions for patrons
- View system reports
- Add/edit book copies
- Manage reservations
- Handle overdue items

### Administrators
- Full system access
- User management
- Book catalog management
- System configuration
- Analytics and reporting
- Bulk operations

## Basic Operations

### Searching for Books

#### Quick Search
Use the search bar on the homepage:
1. Enter keywords (title, author, or subject)
2. Press Enter or click the search icon
3. Browse results in the grid view

#### Advanced Search
For more specific searches:
1. Click "Advanced Search" 
2. Use available filters:
   - **Title**: Exact or partial title match
   - **Author**: Author name(s)
   - **Genre**: Book category
   - **Year**: Publication year range
   - **Language**: Book language
   - **Format**: Physical or Digital
3. Click "Search" to apply filters
4. Use the sort options to organize results

#### Search Tips
- Use quotes for exact phrases: `"data structures"`
- Use wildcards: `program*` matches "programming", "programmer", etc.
- Combine terms: `rust programming beginner`
- Filter by availability to see only books you can check out now

### Viewing Book Information

Click any book from search results to see:
- **Basic Info**: Title, author(s), ISBN, publication details
- **Description**: Book summary and contents
- **Availability**: Current status and location
- **Reviews**: Ratings and comments (if enabled)
- **Related Books**: Similar titles or by same author

### Checking Out Books

#### Self-Service Checkout (if enabled)
1. Find the book you want to check out
2. Click "Check Out" on the book detail page
3. Confirm the due date
4. Click "Confirm Checkout"

#### Staff-Assisted Checkout
1. Bring your books to the circulation desk
2. Present your student/staff ID
3. Library staff will process the checkout
4. Keep your receipt with due dates

### Managing Your Account

#### View Checked Out Books
1. Click "My Account" in the top navigation
2. Select "Current Loans" tab
3. View all books currently checked out:
   - Title and author
   - Due date
   - Renewal options
   - Return status

#### Renewing Books
1. Go to "My Account" > "Current Loans"
2. Find the book you want to renew
3. Click "Renew" if the option is available
4. Confirm the new due date

**Note**: Books can only be renewed if:
- No other user has reserved the book
- You haven't exceeded the maximum renewals (usually 2)
- The book is not overdue

#### Making Reservations
When a book is unavailable:
1. Go to the book's detail page
2. Click "Place Reservation" 
3. You'll be added to the reservation queue
4. You'll be notified when the book becomes available
5. You have 3 days to check out the reserved book

#### Viewing Reservation Queue
1. Go to "My Account" > "Reservations"
2. See all your active reservations:
   - Your position in the queue
   - Estimated availability date
   - Option to cancel reservation

## Advanced Features

### Personal Library
Create collections of your favorite books:
1. Go to "My Account" > "Personal Library"
2. Click "Create New List"
3. Name your list (e.g., "Research Papers", "Pleasure Reading")
4. Add books by searching and clicking "Add to List"
5. Organize and manage your lists

### Reading History
Track your reading activity:
1. Go to "My Account" > "Reading History"
2. View chronological list of all books you've borrowed
3. Filter by date range or subject
4. Export your reading list (if enabled)

### Notifications and Alerts
Stay informed about your account:
- **Due Date Reminders**: 3 days and 1 day before due
- **Overdue Notices**: Daily notifications for late books
- **Reservation Alerts**: When your reserved book is available
- **System Announcements**: Library hours, closures, new features

Manage notifications in "My Account" > "Notification Preferences"

### Mobile Access
Access the system on your phone or tablet:
- Responsive web interface works on all devices
- Mobile app available for iOS and Android (if applicable)
- QR code scanning for quick book lookup (if enabled)

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Log In
**Problem**: Forgotten password or account locked
**Solutions**:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email to receive reset instructions
3. If account is locked, contact library staff
4. Verify you're using the correct email address

### Book Shows as Available but Cannot Check Out
**Problem**: System shows book as available but checkout fails
**Possible Causes**:
- Book may have just been checked out
- Technical hold on the item
- Your account may have reached checkout limits
**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Contact circulation desk for assistance
3. Check your account status in "My Account"

### Cannot Renew Book
**Problem**: Renewal option is not available
**Possible Reasons**:
- Another user has reserved the book
- You've reached maximum renewals
- The book is overdue
- System maintenance
**Solutions**:
1. Check if the book has reservations
2. Return and re-check out if no reservations exist
3. Pay any outstanding fines
4. Contact library staff

### Reservation Not Working
**Problem**: Cannot place a reservation on unavailable book
**Possible Causes**:
- You already have the book checked out
- Maximum reservations reached (usually 5)
- Book is reference-only
**Solutions**:
1. Check your current loans
2. Cancel old reservations if at limit
3. Verify book is eligible for circulation

### Technical Issues
For other technical problems:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different web browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Contact IT support or library staff

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How many books can I check out at once?**
A: This depends on your user type:
- Students: 5 books
- Faculty/Staff: 10 books
- Graduate students: 7 books (if applicable)

**Q: How long can I keep books?**
A: Standard loan periods are:
- Students: 2 weeks
- Faculty/Staff: 4 weeks
- Reference materials: Library use only

**Q: How many times can I renew a book?**
A: You can renew most books up to 2 times, provided no one else has reserved the book.

**Q: What happens if I return a book late?**
A: Late fees may apply:
- Books: $0.25 per day
- Reserve materials: $1.00 per hour
- Maximum fine: $10.00 per item

### Account Management

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings" and click "Change Password"

**Q: Can I update my contact information?**
A: Yes, most contact information can be updated in "Profile Settings". Some fields may require staff verification.

**Q: What if I'm no longer a student/employee?**
A: Contact library administration to update your status. You may need to return all materials and pay any outstanding fines.

### Books and Collections

**Q: Can I suggest books for the library to purchase?**
A: Yes, use the "Suggest a Purchase" feature in the main menu or contact library staff.

**Q: Are there books I cannot check out?**
A: Yes, some restrictions apply:
- Reference materials (use in library only)
- Reserve/course materials (short-term loans)
- Rare books and special collections
- New arrivals (may have holds)

**Q: Can I check out digital/electronic books?**
A: If available, digital resources follow the same rules as physical books. Some may have concurrent user limits.

### System Access

**Q: Can I access the system from off-campus?**
A: Yes, the system is available 24/7 with your login credentials. Some features may require VPN access.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The website works well on mobile browsers. A dedicated app may be available - check your device's app store.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: The system works with all modern browsers:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge
- Mobile browsers

### Help and Support

**Q: Who do I contact for help?**
A: 
- **Technical issues**: IT Help Desk or library systems team
- **Account problems**: Circulation desk
- **General questions**: Reference desk
- **Emergencies**: Main library number

**Q: What are the library hours?**
A: Hours vary by location and season. Check the library website or call for current hours.

**Q: Is training available for new users?**
A: Yes, the library offers:
- Orientation sessions for new students/staff
- Online tutorials and help videos
- One-on-one assistance by appointment
- Written guides and quick reference cards
